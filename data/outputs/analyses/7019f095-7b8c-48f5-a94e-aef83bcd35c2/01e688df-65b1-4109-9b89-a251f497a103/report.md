2. **[High] Strategic Segment Potential Jeopardized by Data Quality Gaps**

3. **[Medium] Optimizing Product Strategy: Balancing Quality, Returns, and Discounting**

**Business Impact:** Poor product quality leads to increased operational costs (returns, customer service) and potential damage to brand reputation. Indiscriminate discounting can diminish perceived product value and directly reduce profitability, particularly for our most profitable product categories. Optimizing these two areas is crucial for maintaining healthy margins and fostering long-term customer loyalty.
